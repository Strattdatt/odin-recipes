Blood Orange Mimosa

Description

Great blood orage-pineapple mimosa for any lazy morning

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup pink sanding sugar, or as needed
  • 1 (750 ml> bottle brut champagne
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup blood orange juice
  • 2 oz. orange liquer

Directions

  1. Dip the rims of 4 chapagne flutes in water. Dip into sanding sugar to coat the rims
  2. Divide the champagne among the flutes. Pour 2 tbsp. pineapple juice and 2 tbsp. blood orange juice into each. Top mimosas with orange liquer.
