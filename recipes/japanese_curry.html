Japanese Curry

Dish Description

Japanese curry is typically made from a paste or bouillon block. Vegetables such as potatoes, carrots and onions are paired with a meat like chicken. The vegetables are boiled in water until cooked and then the meat and curry blocks are added. The mixture is then stirred until the curry block dissolves and thickens. Japanese curry is typically eaten with rice and is very delicious

Ingredients

  • carrots
  • potatoes
  • onions
  • chicken breast
  • rice
  • curry block

Steps

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker
  2. Boil 6 cups of water in large pot
  3. Add vegetables and cooke until softened
  4. Add chicken and curry block
  5. Stir until curry block has fully dissolved and curry has thickend (about 3 mins)
  6. Serve on top of rice and enjoy
