Vaselopita-Greek New Years Cake Recipe

Description

Traditional Greek New Years Day bread. Served as a coffee cake for breakfast. Also the person who receives the quarter inside gets good luck for 1 year!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1.2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup blancyhed slivered almonds
  • 2 tbsp. white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat overn to 350 degrees. Generously grease a 10 inch round cake pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light. etc etc
  3. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven. Remove and sprinkle the nuts and sugar over the cake, then return it to the oven for 20 to 30 additional minutes, until cake springs back to the touch. etc etc
  4. Serve the cake warm. Each person in the family gets a slice starting with the youngest.
